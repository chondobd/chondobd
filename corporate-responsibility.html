Corporate Responsibility


At Fair Trade Group, we understand that our responsibilities go beyond providing customers with fantastic quality and services that offers excellent value and commitment. We are passionate about ensuring a pipeline of skilled employees, for our business and the entire industry.We are committed to ensuring that customers can buy our products, confident that they have been produced under acceptable conditions. We strive to give something back to the community by donating to charities at Group level. 



Finally, we endeavour to minimise our impacts on the environment by complying with the environmental policies, which are consistent with running a responsible business. We make every effort to reduce consumption of valuable resources such as energy, water and fuel. We are constantly developing ways to increase the efficiency and reduce fuel consumption in our transport operations.
