Company Profile



Fair Trade Fabrics Ltd was the start-up company and was incorporated late in the 1997, owned by its principal founders Mr Jasim Ahmed And Mrs Yeasmin Ahmed at 75% and 25% ownership each. Fair Washing Ltd and Fair Zipper & Yarn Dyeing Mills Ltd was incorporated late in the 2005, owned by the principal founders at 75% and 25% ownership each as well. The total production space for Fair Trade Fabrics Ltd is 30,000 Sqr-ft and a production capacity of 50,000 yards per day. We produce- Twills, Canvas and all cotton fancy fabrics. We have 110 Rapiers machine from Japan, Switzerland and Air Jet Picanol from Belgium; hence can produce any kind of blended fabric, as per the requirement of the customer.






Established with the world’s best quality machineries incorporating the latest technology imported form Germany, Italy, U.S.A, Japan, France, Korea, Taiwan, Turkey and Canada. It is run by professionals, skilled technician & management ,who have experience of more then ten (10) years in apparel sector both at home and aboard. The technician , staff and skilled workers are truly professional and fully dedicated to their respective assignment.

The working environment of the factory is very much congenial with neat and clean atmosphere
