FAIR TRADE FABRICS LTD


Fair Trade Fabrics Ltd is a 100% export oriented weaving Factory. Total production space is 43060 out of 58120 Sqr-ft and its located at shafipur, Kaliakoir, Gazipur . We have 110 Rapiers from Japan , Switzerland , Air jet Picanol from Belgium and Different type model of weaving loom like as Sourar & Ishikas and its daily Production Capacity on average of 60,000 yards. We produce different type of Twills, Canvas, Poplin, Sheeting & fancy fabric of all Cotton In addition to this we are in a position to make any kind of blended fabric, any new design from our development or as per the requirement of the customers
