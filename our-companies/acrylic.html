FAIR ACRYLIC YARN PROCESSING LTD


Fair Acrylic Yarn Processing Limited, a concern of Fair Trade Group, which is a fast growing Group in textile sector of Bangladesh,has been established for sophisticated yarn dyeing. The factory is located at shafipur, Kaliakoir, Gazipur. FAYPL was incorporated on as a Private Limited Company in the year 2011 with a view to yarn dyeing of export quality. It will start its trail operation on January 2012. FAYPL has a production space of 44950 square feet with an average production capacity of 65 Ton per day at 22 hour basis. The Company is engaged in yarn dyeing business.The project has been established with the world’s best quality machineries incorporating the latest technology imported from Germany,Italy,Taiwan & China The Company producing various kind of yarn like Acrylic: 20/1, 30/1, 20/2, 30/2)& mixed yarn (50/50, 60/40, 65/35, C/A & C/P).
