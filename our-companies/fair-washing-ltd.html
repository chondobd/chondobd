FAIR WASHING LTD


Fair Washing Ltd (Apparel Unit) is a 100% Export oriented woven Garment Manufacturing unit and main focus on Bottoms, for all ages ranging from children, men’s, lady’s boys & girls.  The company located at shafipur, Kaliakoir, Gazipur. It’s equipped with the world’s best quality machineries incorporating the latest technology imported from Gemany,USA,Italy,France,Korea,Taiwan,Turkey & Canada

 The total production space of the company is 98000sft and 1705 number of sewing machine & equipment engaged in 22 production lines which hold daily production capacity 25000-27000 pcs (8 hours basis).We produce different type of basic pants, 5 pocket, chinos, cargos, patchworks jeans, boot cut jeans or any kind of design given by the customer. Target., Walmart, Centerline (Germany),  Fundamantal, Jennyfer,H & M, Tchibo ( Miles Germany ),Tema (Turkey) Vegotex ( Belgium ),Charles Vogele (Germany ),Big star ( Poland ) is our major buyer.

Fair Washing Ltd (Unit-2: Washing & Dyeing Unit)


Fair Washing Ltd (Washing & Dyeing Unit) is a 100% Export oriented Garment washing & dyeing unit .The company located at shafipur, Kaliakoir, Gazipur. It’s equipped with the world’s best quality machineries with the latest technology imported from Turkey and Hong Kong are the foundation of the project.

Production capacity of the project is 134000sft & 357 number of washing & Dyeing machine with equipment engaged in production and the daily production capacity of the project 60,000pcs garments . The project able to produce all type of garments wash on all type of fabrics as like as stone, enzyme, pigment, acid, Hand brushing, pigment sprays and destroyed washing, wrinkle free, Garment dyeing( pigment & reactive). The project has also leaser blasting and ozone unit and its has also WTP & ETP plant. Target., Walmart, Centerline (Germany),  Fundamantal, Jennyfer,H & M, Tchibo ( Miles Germany ),Tema (Turkey) Vegotex ( Belgium ),Charles Vogele (Germany ),Big star ( Poland ) is our major buyer.


We believe that we are the first washing plant in Bangladesh to have started world best machineries & technologies for quality full garment wash with a high production capacity.
