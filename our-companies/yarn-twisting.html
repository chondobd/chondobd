FAIR YARN TWISTING INDUSTRIES LTD


Fair Yarn Twisting Industries Limited is a sister concern of Fair Trade Group and a fast growing Company in textile sector of Bangladesh. It would be established for sophisticated Cotton & mixed Yarn Twist. The Fair Yarn Twisting Industries ltd established 2011 which is situated at Ratonpur,Shafipur,Kaliakoir, Gazipur and set up world best machineries from Garmany & India. Production space of the company is 1,64,400 sft and average twist capacity of different type of cotton & mixed yarn is 30,000 kg per day on 16 hour basis which all are export quality.

We twist different type of yarn such as (1). 100% Cotton yarn which Count is 20/2, 30/2, 40/2, 20/S, 26/S, 30/S & 36/S  and different type of Mixed Yarn which count is 50/50, 60/40, 65/35, C/A, C/P etc main buyer of the products ,who are engaged in sweater & Knit business in the world like as H&M , Meijer, Orchestra, Penneys Ladie, Pmk, Cardigan,Caroder, Agora, Tao,   Penneys  Men’s, Point Zero,   Luvia,  A W G Mode Center, Siplec,  Primark ladies,  Ch.c Tex, Hypnos, Wal-MartAustralia,Wal-MartCanada,Wal-Mart(Mexico) .
